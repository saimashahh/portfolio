My Story

Hi there, I'm based in Alberta, Canada. With a background in Psychology and Information Systems and a passion for continuous learning, I am transitioning to UX Design.

Earlier in my work history, I learned how to cope with ambiguity and the importance of usability firsthand while working at a payments startup.

Even though my UX Design journey officially started in 2023, it initiated much earlier in the role of content writer.

In my UX Design journey, I have developed design thinking skills coupled with Figma skills to be able to translate a design idea into wireframes for rapid prototyping...

As a designer with a Frontend development focus, I can communicate with frontend developers and design with development in mind.

Outside of Counselling Therapy and UX design, I enjoy hiking and gardening in rural Alberta.

I'm excited about knowing more about your project, contact me and let's talk about it.
