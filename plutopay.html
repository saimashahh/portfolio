PlutoPay Case Study

Welcome to the UX Design Portfolio for the PlutoPay Project

The Problem

Competitive analysis and research showed that while the financial app market is saturated, it lacks products catering to 'Digital nomads' (19-40 years old).

  • Need to exchange currency quickly and efficiently.
  • Payments in multiple currencies.
  • Challenges include exchange rate variability, travel issues, language barriers, and security concerns.

Users need an efficient and secure online payment solution for shopping, travel, money transfers, and budget management.

The Process

The product development process included user research, persona creation, and iterative testing. Tools included card sorting, user interviews, affinity mapping, and wireframe design.
